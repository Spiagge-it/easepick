## Tokens format

_Tokens are case-sensitive._

| | Token | Output
| --- | :---: |
| Day of Month | D |	1 2 … 30 31 
| | DD | 01 02 … 30 31
| Month |	M |	1 2 … 11 12
| | MM | 01 02 … 11 12
| | MMM | Jan Feb … Nov Dec
| | MMMM | January February … November December
| Year | YY | 70 71 … 29 30
| | YYYY | 1970 1971 … 2029 2030
| Hours (24 hour time) | H | 0 1 … 22 23
| | HH | 01 02 … 22 23
| Minutes | m | 1 2 … 58 59
| | mm | 01 02 … 58 59
| Seconds | s | 1 2 … 58 59
| | ss | 01 02 … 58 59
